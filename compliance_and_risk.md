# Compliance & Risk Disclosure

This document is critical if you ever scale capital or involve third parties.

---

## 1. Nature of the System

- This is a **rule-based trading system**
- It does NOT guarantee profits
- Historical performance is not indicative of future results

---

## 2. Key Risks

### Market Risk
- Gap risk
- Volatility expansion
- Tail events

### Strategy Risk
- Regime change
- Prolonged drawdowns
- Correlated losses

### Operational Risk
- Data errors
- API failures
- Execution latency

---

## 3. Risk Controls Implemented

- Max risk per trade
- Weekly drawdown kill-switch
- No overnight discretionary overrides

---

## 4. Capital Scaling Warning

> Capital should only be scaled after:
- Minimum 300 live paper trades
- Stable drawdown distribution
- No rule changes mid-cycle

---

## 5. Regulatory Note (India)

- You are responsible for SEBI compliance
- This system is NOT an investment advisory product
- Do not solicit capital without registration
